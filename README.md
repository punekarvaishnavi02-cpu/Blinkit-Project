# 📊 Blinkit Sales Performance and Customer Behavior Analysis

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail transactional data from Blinkit to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and inventory distribution across different outlet types and locations. The goal is to convert raw data into meaningful insights that support better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales performance across product categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate outlet performance by location and size
- Identify inventory imbalance issues
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **SQL** – Data extraction, cleaning, aggregation, and analysis  
- **Excel** – Data validation and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization  
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and measures  

---

## 📂 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Handled missing values
- Removed duplicates
- Standardized categorical variables
- Ensured data consistency for analysis

---

### 2. SQL Analysis Performed
- Total sales by product category  
- Outlet-wise performance analysis  
- Customer rating distribution  
- Identification of top and low-performing products  

---

### 3. Power BI Dashboard
Created an interactive dashboard with:
- Outlet Type Filter  
- Location Tier Filter  
- Product Category Filter  
- Dynamic KPI cards and visuals  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Sales  
- Number of Items Sold  
- Average Sales per Transaction  
- Average Customer Rating  
- Outlet Performance by Size and Location  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- FMCG categories like snacks and fruits & vegetables generated the highest sales  
- Tier 3 cities showed strong demand and growth potential  
- Medium-sized outlets performed best overall  
- Customers preferred low-fat and essential daily-use products  
- Inventory imbalance was observed across several product categories  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Improve inventory forecasting for high-demand products  
- Increase focus on Tier 3 cities for marketing and expansion  
- Expand medium-sized outlets for better ROI  
- Introduce promotions for underperforming categories  
- Optimize stock management using demand forecasting  

---

## 📈 Outcome
- Identified top-performing product categories  
- Improved understanding of customer buying behavior  
- Highlighted inventory inefficiencies  
- Provided actionable insights for business growth
